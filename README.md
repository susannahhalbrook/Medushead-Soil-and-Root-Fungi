# Medushead-Soil-and-Root-Fungi
Analysis of soil and root endosphere fungal microbiome of a prolific invasive grass, Medusahead, and two co-occuring local grasses, Avena barbata and Bromus tectorum, across precipitation levels in northern California.

See associated publication for detailed sampling methods. 

At the six sites surveyed in northern California in 2021, roots and soil samples were collected from three grass species. This analysis includes community assessment from amplicon sequencing data and soil nutrient analysis.
